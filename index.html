About Empulse

Empulse 2025, the flagship fest of E-Cell Yukta, is set to be an exciting platform for aspiring entrepreneurs. Participants will have unique opportunities to test their business acumen, problem-solving skills, and creativity. The fest will also feature engaging speaker sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities, making it a must-attend for budding entrepreneurs.

Major Events at Empulse 2025

Investify - Money Multiplier – A strategic finance-based competition to test investment and money management skills.

Startup Showcase – A platform for startups to present their innovative ideas and gain exposure.

Business Hackathon – A fast-paced problem-solving competition focused on real-world business challenges.

Escape Rooms – A fun, team-based challenge that tests problem-solving and critical thinking.

Taxiwars Startup Scaling Contest – A strategic competition to develop growth plans for Taxiwars, a ride-sharing startup.
